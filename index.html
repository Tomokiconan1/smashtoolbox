How to Play/Assumptions

Step 1: Choose Your Character

Click on the character you will be using (the one shielding the attack) and press "Start".

Step 2: Answer Questions

Select the correct answer before timer runs out (~15 seconds).

Step 3: Check Results

Click on "View Explanations" to review which questions you got correct/incorrect.

Assumptions (Version 1.0)

  • Attacker is close enough for all oos options to hit.
  • Attacker is on the FRONT SIDE after shielding attack.
  • Attacker is on the GROUND after shielding attack.
  • Choose guaranteed oos punish with the SLOWEST startup.
  • If nothing works, choose "No Guaranteed Punish".
  • Footstools are not included as an oos option.
